## What it is

QDROdl is a legal-tech web app built to turn a structured intake into a consistent QDRO/DRO document package.

## Why it exists

QDRO workflows are detail-heavy. The goal is simple: reduce rework, standardize outputs, and make the process easier to repeat and support.

## Tech

- Next.js
- TypeScript
- PostgreSQL
- Prisma
- Stripe
- Document automation / templating workflows
